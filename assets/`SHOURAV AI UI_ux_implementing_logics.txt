`SHOURAV AI THEME`

------------------

## 1. CORE DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Design a **luxury, voice-first, gesture-aware AI interface** named **SHOURAV A.I.**, inspired by:

* **Nothing OS minimal discipline**
* **High-end automotive AI (Mercedes / Tesla / Audi MMI)**
* **Human-like calm breathing**
* **Obsidian black + gold/chrome precision**
* **Zero clutter, zero cartoonism**

This is **not a chatbot UI**.
This is a **sentient control surface**.

  * Chrome Gold (warm)
  * Deep Amber

* **Text:** Soft off-white (never pure white)

### Material Language

* Glassy matte surfaces
* Soft inner glow (never outer glow)
* Metallic rings with **realistic depth**
* No gradients unless radial and subtle
